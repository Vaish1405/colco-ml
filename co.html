Colombia Research Team

Research involves meeting farmers in Colombia and collected necessary data for the project.

About Us

As students on an international research trip to Colombia, our interdisciplinary fieldwork examines small-scale farming in the Coffee region, with a specific emphasis on Cacao cultivation. We will use Geographical Information Systems (GIS) to digitize and map data, creating visualizations of crop distribution and environmental patterns related to cocoa plants. By integrating field-collected data with GIS tools, our work provides valuable insights that serve as a foundational resource for decision-making. The collected data will be used by other teams working on this project to develop a deep-learning-based disease detection model and conduct a thorough biological analysis offering clear insights into farmers' crops and showcasing ways to optimize their crop growth. This opportunity fosters collaboration with Colombian farmers, promoting cross-cultural exchange while advancing farming techniques, utilizing GIS, and promoting sustainability.
