Disease Detection

Leveraging the power of deep learning to accurately detect diseases affecting cacao pods.

About our Project

Our team uses cutting-edge deep learning models, including convolutional neural networks (CNNs), to detect disease symptoms on cocoa pods. By processing images with advanced techniques and augmentations, we improve diagnostic accuracy and robustness. With feature extraction focusing on color, texture, and lesion patterns, our approach helps farmers detect diseases early and protect their crops.

Healthy Cocoa Pod

Healthy cocoa pods exhibit uniform color, smooth texture, and no signs of lesions.

Diseased Cocoa Pod (Fito)

Diseased cocoa pods display discoloration, rough texture, and visible signs of lesions.
